Scotland's Covid-19 Data

This site hastily thrown together by @RobertGauld, the code is available at https://github.com/robertgauld/scotland-covid19-data.

Thanks to:

  • @watty62 for collecting and preprocessing the Scotland data at https://github.com/watty62/Scot_covid19, thanks to his further work this data is now available from the Scottish Government at https://github.com/DataScienceScotland/COVID-19-Management-Information.
    Data upto Wednesday 31 March 2021 is currently available and displayed.
  • @tom_e_white for collecting and preprocessing the UK data at https://github.com/tomwhite/covid-19-uk-data and to geeogi for keeping it going at https://github.com/geeogi/covid-19-uk-data. This data is now gathered from https://coronavirus.data.gov.uk.
    Data upto Wednesday 31 March 2021 is currently available and displayed.
  • Also includes a Google dataset: Google LLC "Google COVID-19 Community Mobility Reports". https://www.google.com/covid19/mobility/ Accessed 2021-04-01.

Download the current site and raw data.

  • https://covidvisualizer.com/country?iso=GB
  • https://www.covidlive.co.uk/

  • https://91-divoc.com/pages/covid-visualization/
